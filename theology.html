Department of Theology

The Department of Theology is the branch of the Royal Cyprian Academy responsible for preserving the Old and New Testaments and all beautiful, insightful or otherwise important texts connected therewith.


Old and New Testaments

  • The official Cyprian version of the Old Testament is the Septuagint as published by the Apostolic Press.
  • The official Cyprian version of the New Testament is the Greek text thereof, as published by the Apostolic Press.
  • The official Cyprian translation of the Old and New Testaments into English is the Revised Standard Version, specifically the Anglicised version of the 1962 text.
