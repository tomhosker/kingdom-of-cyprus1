His Majesty The King

His Majesty The King is the head of state of the Kingdom of Cyprus, as well as duke, earl, baron and lord-of-the-manor of Nicosia. The office of king is vacant at present, and the powers thereof discharged by a regent.

His Majesty is sometimes referred to in writing using the abbreviations Rex and R.
