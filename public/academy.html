Royal Cyprian Academy

His Majesty's Royal Cyprian Academy is a body appointed by His Majesty, responsible for maintaining and regulating the Kingdom's culture and institutions of learning.


Faculty of Liberal Arts

The Faculty of Liberal Arts is divided into departments for each of the three Cyprian liberal arts.

  • Department of Theology,
  • Department of Philosophy,
  • Department of Mathematics.

Faculty of Fine Arts

The Faculty of Fine Arts is responsible for maintaining Hosker's Canons.


Faculty of Useful Arts

The Faculty of Useful Arts concerns all those arts necessary for the wealth and flourishing of mankind.
